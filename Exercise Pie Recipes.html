Apple Pie

INGREDIENTS:

1/3 cup packed light brown sugar

1/3 cup white sugar

1 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon lemon juice

1/3 teaspoon ground cinnamon

7 1/2 cups peeleds,cored and sliced apples

1 cup raisins

1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie

1 egg

PREPARATION:

Preheat over 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Spary deep dish pie plate with cooking spary.

Combine white sugar, light brown sugar, flour, lemon, cinnamon, and mix well. Add apples raisins to sugar mixture; stir until fruit is well coated.

Spoon apple mixture into pie crust. Place second piecrust on top of filling, and trim edges. Lightly glaze top of pie with a beaten egg, then sprinkled with a little sugar.

Bake till gloden brown, about 35 to 40 minutes. Place on a wire rack, and cool 30 minutes.
